How Healthcare Language handles system, partner, and inquiry-related information.

This policy explains how Healthcare Language may collect, use, and protect information for website inquiries, system evaluation, and future product operations.

Scope

Website inquiries, product evaluation, partner communication, and future system operations.

Control model

Administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards aligned with regulated healthcare environments.

Contact point

Email: enquiry@healthcarelanguage.ca

01

Information we collect

We may collect contact details, organization details, inquiry content, uploaded materials, language-service requirements, product evaluation information, and limited operational data needed to support communication, security, and service planning.

02

How we use it

We use information to respond to inquiries, support product access, improve service quality, maintain system security, and help healthcare and government stakeholders evaluate Healthcare Language.

03

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Limited information may be shared with authorized infrastructure, hosting, translation, or service partners where necessary to operate the system or support contracted services.

04

Retention

Information may be retained for customer relationship management, compliance, system operations, security review, and regulated service support as required by law or contract.

05

Security controls

We apply administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards appropriate to system risk, including access control, encryption, backup, monitoring, audit support, and governed review workflows.

06

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact enquiry@healthcarelanguage.ca.
